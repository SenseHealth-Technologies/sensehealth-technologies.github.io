Our Story: Key Milestones

From a bold idea to a revolutionary product, our mission has always been clear: make proactive health monitoring accessible to everyone.

2022 - The Spark

Conceptualization and initial research into non-invasive sensor technology at IIT.

Early 2023 - First Prototype

Development of the first-generation fingertip device and foundational AI algorithms.

Late 2023 - Seed Funding

Secured initial funding to accelerate R&D and filed key technology patents.

2024 - Clinical Validation

Began rigorous testing and validation against clinical gold standards.

2025 - Launching Soon

Preparing for commercial launch and seeking final regulatory approvals.
